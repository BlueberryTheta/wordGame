Inqaily respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Usage data (pages viewed, basic interactions) for analytics.

Cookies and Advertising

We use cookies and similar technologies for functionality and analytics. This site also uses Google AdSense to show ads.

  • Third‑party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and/or other websites.
  • Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also visit aboutads.info to opt out of third‑party vendors’ cookies for interest‑based advertising.

AI Services

To operate the game, we send limited data to an AI service provider (OpenAI). Specifically:

  • When you ask a question, the question text and the secret word are sent to the AI to generate a brief answer.
  • When you enter a guess, the guessed token may be sent to the AI to help validate whether it is a common English word.

We use this data solely to provide the game’s functionality.

Server Logs and Hosting

We may record limited request information for security and debugging (e.g., the day key, questions/guesses you submit, timestamps). Our hosting provider may also process IP addresses and basic request metadata in access logs to deliver and protect the service.
