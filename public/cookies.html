We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the site, remember preferences, and understand usage. Advertising partners (like Google) may also set cookies to show relevant ads.

Types of Cookies

  • Preferences/local storage: to remember game state and settings in your browser.
  • Analytics: to measure aggregate usage.
  • Advertising: set by third‑party partners to show and measure ads.

Your Choices
